All New SUVs

SUV stands for Sport Utility Vehicle, a type of vehicle that combines passenger car features like ample space with off-road vehicle capabilities such as high ground clearance and rugged design. These vehicles are often built with a body-on-frame construction and a four-wheel-drive system, making them suitable for navigating challenging terrain.
