Toyota Tundra

The Toyota Tundra is known for its legendary reliability and powerful performance, recently redesigned to feature a standard i-FORCE twin-turbo V6 engine, offering increased efficiency and torque compared to previous generations. It is built on a high-strength box steel frame and incorporates a sophisticated coil-spring rear suspension to balance heavy-duty capability with improved ride quality. The Tundra offers rugged off-road trims and advanced towing technology, solidifying its place as a dependable and capable full-size truck.
