Aston Martin DB12

The Aston Martin DB12 is the world's first Super Tourer, fusing exhilarating performance with exceptional luxury. It is powered by a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that produces 671 horsepower, marking a significant step up from its predecessor. With a refined chassis, advanced electronic stability systems, and an exquisitely handcrafted interior, the DB12 delivers both the explosive power of a supercar and the effortless cruising ability of a grand tourer.
