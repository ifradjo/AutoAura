McLaren 720S

The McLaren 720S is defined by its relentless pursuit of performance and its distinctive aerodynamic design. Built around a carbon fibre Monocage II chassis, it is powered by a ferocious 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 710 horsepower (720 PS), capable of reaching 0-60 mph in a blinding 2.8 seconds. Its innovative Proactive Chassis Control II system allows it to balance track precision with exceptional ride comfort.
