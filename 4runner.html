Toyota 4Runner

The Toyota 4Runner is celebrated for its body-on-frame construction, making it one of the most durable and off-road-capable SUVs on the market. Powered by a dependable 4.0-liter V6 engine, it offers legendary reliability and features like the optional Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS) for enhanced articulation off-road. Its rugged design, spacious interior, and proven longevity appeal directly to adventure-seekers and families needing a highly capable vehicle.
